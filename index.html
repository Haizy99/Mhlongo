About

I'm a QA automation engineer who found my way into software development through a love of finding out how things break — and then figuring out how to stop them from breaking again. My day-to-day involves building end-to-end test suites with Playwright, verifying fixes down to the database and API level, and working inside a low-code platform (Shesha) that sits closer to real product code than most testing roles get to.

I bring a detail-oriented mindset, a strong work ethic, and a genuine curiosity about the systems I test — not just whether they pass, but why they might fail. I'm currently expanding beyond QA into full-stack development, and I'm looking for a team where I can keep growing while helping ship dependable software.
